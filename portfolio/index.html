ABOUT ME

Xinamayca de Cuba, 20 years old

Hi, I'm Xinamayca de Cuba, and I come from the beautiful island of Aruba. I'm 20 years old and currently studying ICT & Media Design at Fontys in Eindhoven. I love being creative, bringing fresh ideas to the table, and challenging myself to think outside the box.

At Fontys, I’ve developed skills in UX design, branding, prototyping, and coding with tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and GSAP. One of my proudest achievements was leading a branding project for GO IT, where my team and I created a full brand identity, from the logo to the website.

In the future, I hope to combine my passion for fitness and technology by joining the Dutch military, contributing to innovative solutions in a field that aligns with my personal values. I thrive in collaborative environments where I can solve problems, learn, and grow as a professional.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy discovering new music, lifting weights, and experimenting with photography. I believe in the importance of having fun while working hard, and as my teacher says, "make cool shit." and that’s exactly what I aim to do with every project I take on.
