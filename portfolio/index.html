ABOUT ME

Hi! I’m Xinamayca, a 20-year-old designer from the beautiful island of Aruba. I’m currently studying ICT & Media Design at Fontys in Eindhoven, where I get to explore into my passion for creativity, technology, and solving real-world problems.

I’m all about user experience design, and I enjoy using design thinking to create smart, user-centered solutions. I especially love working on projects that solve real-life challenges and push me to think differently and creatively.

Outside of school, I enjoy lifting weights and discovering new music. In the future, I hope to combine my passion for fitness and technology by joining the Dutch military, contributing to innovative solutions in a field that aligns with my personal values.

If you enjoyed my projects, don’t be afraid to connect with me on LinkedIn! I’d love to hear from you.
